About Ms Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand. He was passionate about cricket from a young age and quickly rose through the ranks with his natural talent, strong work ethics.His parents hailed from Lwali village in Uttar Pradesh (now Uttarakhand) and he was the youngest of three children.His family spells the surname as "Mahi".Dhoni did his schooling from DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir, where he started playing football as a goalkeeper, but later moved to play cricket on the suggestion of his coach Keshav Banerjee.From 2001 to 2003, Dhoni worked as a Travelling Ticket Examiner (TTE) at Kharagpur under South Eastern Railway zone of Indian Railways

Achievements

  • ICC Cricket World Cup 2011
  • ICC World Twenty20 Cup 2007
  • Champions Trophy 2013
  • 300 ODI dismissals
  • Recipient of Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award
  • Padma Shri
  • Padma Bhushan

Records

  1. The only Captain in cricketing history to win ICC World Cup, Champions Trophy, and ICC T20 World Cup.
  2. Dhoni holds the record of highest runs in an ODI innings as a wicket-keeper – 183.
  3. The only Indian Captain to lead the team for 200 ODI matches
  4. the first batsman to cross 10K runs with an average of more than 50.
  5. The former skipper of India is the fastest player to reach the No.1 spot in the ICC batsman list. He entered the first position in just 42 innings.
  6. MSD holds the record of most number of sixes by a Captain in International cricket – 204.
  7. The fastest stumping record in cricket is held by MS Dhoni, who stumped Keemo Paul in just 0.08 seconds during an ODI match
